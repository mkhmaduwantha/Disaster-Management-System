About

Who we are and what is this about

We are a team of 4 members studying at University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. We build this app to help people. The app is developed to work only in Sri Laka. This app will provide mainly,

  • Disaster database with disaster likelyhood
  • Emergency map with disaster locations and many details
  • Several communication methods between users

This app is made for use in low data mode without depending on the mobile performance. This will support considerable amount of concurrent users.
